The Context

A crisis hiding in plain sight

Food waste and food poverty exist side by side. Millions go hungry while supermarkets discard tonnes of perfectly good products every year.

Food Poverty in Ireland

1 in 11 people live in food poverty, and 25% of families worry about being unable to provide enough food for their children. 83% of adults believe grocery costs are unacceptably high, and 48% feel financial pressure from rising food prices.

Environmental Devastation

Food waste accounts for approximately 10% of global greenhouse gas emissions — generating twice the emissions of the entire aviation industry. Decomposing food releases methane, a gas 25 times stronger than CO₂, while the wasted food had already consumed 18% of farmland, 19% of fertilisers, and 21% of freshwater.

Pharmaceutical & Cosmetic Waste

Even when handled properly, disposing of unsold pharmaceutical and cosmetic products is energy-intensive — requiring recollection, transportation, and specialised processing. Prevention is far more sustainable than disposal.

Our Solution

Meet Vireo

An online marketplace for near-expiry products and items safe for consumption but unsuitable for full-price sale. We digitalise existing in-store discounts, making them visible and searchable for everyone.

Discounts of 75–90% on near-expiry products already exist in stores. But customers don't know what's discounted, in which stores, or on what days. Vireo solves this information gap.

Impact

Creating lasting change

Vireo delivers impact because it addresses the primary barrier to food security: affordability. By making nutritious food, medicine, and personal care products more accessible, we create ripple effects across communities.

🌱 Social Impact

Improves food security by giving households reliable access to affordable essential goods.

Lowers financial strain — estimated to cut grocery bills by around 50%.

Reduces reliance on food banks and charities by offering a dignified, market-based alternative.

Enables low-income families to maintain a healthy, protein-rich diet.

Removes the psychological barrier: users are treated as customers, not aid recipients, preserving dignity and independence.

🌍 Environmental Impact

Prevents food waste, reducing methane emissions from decomposing food in landfills.

Conserves the land, water, and energy used to produce food that would otherwise be discarded.

Reduces the environmental footprint of pharmaceutical and cosmetic disposal — which requires energy-intensive recollection, transport, and processing.

Makes more efficient use of existing agricultural resources — more people fed from the same production level.

💚 Psychological Comfort

For many people, food insecurity comes with shame, stress, and a fear of being judged — which keeps them from seeking help. Vireo removes this barrier by providing a familiar shopping experience. Users are treated as customers rather than recipients of aid, preserving their sense of dignity, independence, and personal choice while delivering meaningful support.

Business Model

Built to last & scale

Business Model

Vireo operates as a self-sustaining social enterprise. A small commission is charged to retailers on each transaction — creating a model that does not depend on external funding or donations.

Customers never pay extra. Prices listed on the platform must match the in-store price to ensure fairness and prevent price discrimination.

Highly Scalable

1

The platform is easily replicable across regions. Food waste, affordability challenges, and near-expiry discounting practices exist in most countries, which means that the core model can be transferred with minimal adaptation.

2

Vireo uses existing retail inventory, keeping marginal costs low as the platform grows.

3

Network effects accelerate growth: as more retailers and consumers join, the platform becomes more valuable for everyone.

4

The technology is modular, so new features and partnerships can be added easily without disrupting the existing platform.

5

Data-driven optimisation enables continuous improvement at scale — from smarter pricing suggestions to more efficient retailer notifications.

Community & charity partnerships

Vireo will collaborate with local charities and community organisations to ensure the platform reaches the people who need it most — including those who may not typically use apps.

Eligible families will receive priority access to newly listed products. If items are not claimed within a defined period, they are released to the general public — ensuring that households facing the greatest need are served first.
